The frameworks we cover

Proof of Work

A banking engagement, end to end

Commercial Bank · NFRS 9 Migration

NAS to full NFRS, including ECL — three weeks ahead of the NRB deadline

Challenge
A commercial bank faced a tight NRB deadline to migrate its financial statements from NAS to full NFRS, including an NFRS 9 expected-credit-loss model it had never operated before.
Solution
Gap analysis, a rebuilt chart of accounts mapped to NRB formats, and an ECL model aligned with both NFRS 9 and NRB provisioning directives — built alongside the bank’s finance team so it remained operable after handover.
Result
Fully compliant statements delivered three weeks ahead of the regulatory deadline, with zero audit findings.
