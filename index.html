Banking · NFRS 9

Commercial bank NFRS migration, delivered three weeks early

Challenge
A commercial bank faced a tight NRB deadline to migrate financial statements from NAS to full NFRS, including NFRS 9 expected-credit-loss modelling.
Solution
Gap analysis, a rebuilt chart of accounts, and an ECL model aligned with NFRS 9 and NRB directives — run alongside the bank’s own finance team.
Result
Fully compliant statements delivered three weeks ahead of the regulatory deadline, with zero audit findings.
