Case Studies

Real Engagements. Measurable Outcomes.

A look at how we've helped clients navigate complex regulatory and financial challenges.

Banking · NFRS

Banking Sector NFRS Implementation

Challenge
A commercial bank faced a tight NRB deadline to migrate financial statements from NAS to full NFRS, including NFRS 9 ECL modelling.
Solution
We ran a gap analysis, rebuilt the chart of accounts, and implemented an ECL model aligned with NFRS 9 and NRB directives.
Result
Fully compliant statements delivered three weeks ahead of the regulatory deadline with zero audit findings.
Manufacturing · Tax

Manufacturing Tax Optimization

Challenge
A mid-size manufacturer was overpaying corporate tax due to missed allowable deductions and inefficient structuring.
Solution
We conducted a full tax health-check, restructured intercompany arrangements, and filed amended returns within IRD guidelines.
Result
Achieved a 22% reduction in effective tax rate through fully legitimate, documented optimization.
Hydropower · Advisory

Hydropower Financial Restructuring

Challenge
A hydropower developer needed to restructure debt and equity ahead of a refinancing round with international lenders.
Solution
We prepared a bankable financial model, performed a business valuation, and supported lender due diligence end-to-end.
Result
Secured refinancing at improved terms, reducing the project's weighted cost of capital by 1.8 percentage points.
